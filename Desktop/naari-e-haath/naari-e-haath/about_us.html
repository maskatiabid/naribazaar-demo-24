About Us

Naari-E-Haath is a marketplace dedicated to empowering women entrepreneurs in Maharashtra and Gujarat. Our mission is to provide a platform where women can showcase and sell their unique products, whether it be handicrafts, clothing, food, or any other wonderful creation. We believe in the power of women to drive economic change and create sustainable livelihoods for themselves and their families. By connecting sellers with buyers, we aim to foster a community of women supporting women.

At Naari-E-Haath, we are passionate about making entrepreneurship more accessible to women, enabling them to take charge of their financial independence while promoting their products to a larger audience.
